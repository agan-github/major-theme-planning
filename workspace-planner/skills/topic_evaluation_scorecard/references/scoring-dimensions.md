# Scoring Dimensions

Use a 1 to 5 scale.

- 5 = strong advantage
- 4 = solid and usable
- 3 = mixed but repairable
- 2 = weak with structural problems
- 1 = not currently viable

## High-value questions

### Source grounding
Is there a real anchor case and evidence chain?

### Audience entry
Can the target audience enter and understand the topic?

### Distinctiveness
Is the angle generic or institutionally distinctive?

### Evidence and scene strength
Can the topic be shown as well as explained?

### Execution feasibility
Is access and coordination realistic?

### Amplification potential
Will scaling strengthen or distort the topic?

### Strategic fit
Does the topic fit mission, timing, and institutional strength?
